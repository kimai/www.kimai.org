Time Tracking for Non-Profit Organizations ✨

Less administrative burden, so your mission stays in focus

Today we say thank you to the many NGOs and NPOs:

  • Charitable and philanthropic organizations
  • Public and independent schools, universities, community colleges
  • Public safety agencies, emergency services, disaster relief, fire departments, civil protection
  • Conservation, sports, and all other clubs that foster community and teach skills
  • Social services, elderly care, and youth facilities
  • … and the many, many others we forgot to mention

You give your time, create small and great wonders, and enrich the lives of so many: Thank you for being there ❤️

As an organization grows, donations increase, and the first employees come on board, the need for structured time tracking becomes clear. Documenting workflows transparently helps identify opportunities for improvement early on – freeing up resources for your actual mission while laying the groundwork for transparent accountability reports.

Features that NPOs appreciate

Kimai helps many non-profits gain insight into their projects while simplifying working time management. We have built a time tracking tool that speaks your language – simple, reliable, GDPR-compliant, and designed to make your daily work run more smoothly.
