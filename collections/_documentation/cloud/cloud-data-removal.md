I do not make any money from your private data and for data minimisation reasons,
**unused free accounts** will be irretrievably deleted after a few weeks waiting period. 

See [Terms of service]({% link _pages/terms-cloud.md %}) for the exact duration.

### Why data is deleted

Many people quietly accept that using the internet is a threat to their privacy; 
that their private information is sold for money and abused by Big Tech.
Not with me: I don't do that. I fight against it! I do everything I can to protect your privacy.
One of our strongest weapons in protecting your privacy is deleting your data when you're no longer using the product.

I believe that **deleting unused data is the best way to prove to everyone that collecting user accounts is not part of the business**.
I want to provide you with a safe working environment. Privacy first is one of my top rules.
There is no investor behind me, so I do not have and do not want to fake numbers with expired accounts! I do make my own rules.

And one of these rules is: **If an account is not used for one month, it will be removed.**

The system will let you know about an upcoming deletion with a warning that "Your cloud is unused - Data deletion is imminent" email.
After receiving this email, you have a short timeframe of a few days to log in and create a subscription, in order to create new timesheets.
If you fail to do so, your cloud will be irretrievable/permanently deleted.

**This process only applies to free users.** Paying customers pay me to host Kimai - their data is protected and will not be touched.

## Can yo restore my deleted data?

Short and simple answer: **NO.**

If you, for some reason, werenÄt aware of that rule and your data was deleted, then it is gone.
That is the entire purpose of deleting data in a privacy conform manner.

There is no backup of your deleted data, it cannot be restored, even if you offer to pay for it.

### Missing your login / cloud?

You can ask for details, but unfortunately there is no way I can provide them.
If you don't use Kimai, your data will be completely deleted (hello GDPR).
I don't know if you had an account in the past.
If it is not there, but you are sure it existed, then it has been removed by our privacy processes.

**You are always welcome to register a new account and new cloud!**

But please make sure that you can receive and actually read the transactional system-emails next time.
I don't spam, you just get important updates about your cloud.

Unsubscribing from our system emails is not only a bad idea, it's a violation of the TOS.

### Warning emails

Kimai will send you warning emails that your cloud is unused with more information if the following applies:

- after the trial period has expired and you are on the unpaid plan
- the last timesheet was created more than 30 days ago

If you don't need your Kimai cloud anymore, you don't have to do anything: shortly after sending the warning-email,
your cloud and your account will be completely deleted.

### Data protection for paying customer

Data for paying accounts is secure.

Paying customers are protected and their data will never be touched by this process, while having an active subscription.

Once a subscription is canceled and expired, the above rules apply to you. 

We do NOT keep your data, even if you paid us in the past to do so.

Yes we have been asked if we keep the data to archive them for 7 years, to match regulative laws in some countries.
And we are happy to do so if you pay us for that, but if you don't: please  [download your latest backup]({% link _documentation/cloud/cloud-backups.md %}), it includes your entire cloud.
