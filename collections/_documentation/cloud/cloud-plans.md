---
title: Plan change
description: How to upgrade or downgrade your current plan
---

### Change to a higher plan

**When:** Upgrading to a higher-priced plan is possible at any time and takes effect immediately.

**How:** This is currently not possible online — please contact support.

**Note:** The old Plus and Premium plans were deprecated at the end of 2023. Customers on those plans need to switch to one of the current plans.

### Change to a lower plan

**When:** Downgrading to a lower-priced plan is possible at any time and takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

**How:** This is currently not possible online — please contact support.

**Note:** The old Plus and Premium plans were deprecated at the end of 2023. Customers on those plans need to switch to one of the current plans.

### Cancel your subscription

You may cancel your subscription up to the last day before the renewal date.

For more information, see [Payments]({% link _documentation/cloud/cloud-payment.md %}).
