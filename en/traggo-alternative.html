Kimai is open source

Kimai is the #1 open-source time-tracking software. With it, you're not only investing in a more secure and cost-efficient solution, but you're also embracing unparalleled transparency and flexibility.

Host the on-premise edition

Kimai prioritizes data privacy and information security, providing an option for self-hosting via its open-source/on-premise edition. Choosing on-premises software like Kimai ensures a height level of security, allowing businesses to adhere to stringent data protection regulations with confidence. Beyond its security features, Kimai offers the adaptability to customize to specific needs, providing a degree of control over data processing and storage that's sometimes elusive in cloud solutions.

Additionally, for businesses aiming to integrate other applications, Kimai comes equipped with a robust API to facilitate such integrations seamlessly.

Data protection

If you decide to go with the cloud edition, you can be sure that your data is safe. With the headquarters located in Austria (EU), it is GDPR-compliant by default and all data is stored in Germany (Hetzner Cloud, Falkenstein).

Conclusion

In today's digital age, data security is paramount. Kimai promises secure data protection and state-of-the-art encryption, ensuring that your business's sensitive information remains confidential.

Kimai offers a well-rounded solution that caters to a variety of workflows and use-cases for most project-based teams and companies. Its ease of use, integration capabilities, and commitment to data security and privacy make it a top choice for businesses seeking an efficient time-tracking software.
