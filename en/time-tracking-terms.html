Time Log

A time log is a systematic record that documents the duration spent on various tasks and activities within a specific timeframe, serving as a tool for tracking and analyzing time usage.

Time Tracker

A time-tracker is a software tool or application designed to automatically or manually record and monitor how time is spent on various activities, tasks, or projects, helping individuals and organizations measure and analyze their time usage.
