Clockodo: a German time-tracking solution

Clockodo has established itself as a popular time-tracking solution, particularly in German-speaking markets. It offers an intuitive interface and solid features for businesses managing their time tracking needs, including flexible absence workflow.

However, as your organization grows, you may be looking for a more flexible, cost-effective alternative that offers greater control over your data. That’s where Kimai comes in.

Time Tracking Requirements on German-Speaking Markets

Companies in Austria, Germany, and other German-speaking regions face specific regulatory requirements when it comes to time tracking.

  • Zeiterfassungspflicht (Time Recording Obligation) is a fundamental requirement that mandates accurate tracking of working hours. German labor law, for example, requires employers to record the start and end times of daily work periods to ensure compliance with working time regulations and proper wage payment.
  • GDPR Compliance is non-negotiable for any organization handling employee data in the EU. It sets stringent standards for data collection, storage, and processing.
  • Time tracking systems must ensure that sensitive employee information is protected according to these regulations. Kimai is fully compliant with both requirements.
  • Built with privacy and security at its core, Kimai ensures that your time tracking data is handled according to the highest European data protection standards. With our German servers, GDPR compliance is built into our platform by design, not added as an afterthought.
