Lemonsqueezy Invoices

How to generate invoices for your plugin subscriptions from Lemonsqueezy

LemonSqueezy allows customers to generate invoices for orders and subscription payments. Customers can generate invoices through the “My Orders” page in the customer portal, specifically within the billing history section for subscription payments.

To do this, log in to your account at https://app.lemonsqueezy.com/my-orders using the email associated with the purchase, navigate to the relevant order or subscription, and access the invoice from the billing history.

See this Lemonsqueezy documentation for more details.
