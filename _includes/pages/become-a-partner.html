The relationship, in plain terms

What recognition is — and isn't

{% capture text %} Recognition as an Official Kimai Partner is **non-exclusive**. Kimai may recognise any number of partners in the same region or specialism, and partners remain free to work with other software and vendors. The designation is **reviewed periodically**. It reflects current standing rather than a permanent title, and is **revocable** — for misrepresentation of the relationship, a sustained drop in quality, or conduct that breaches the program's expectations. Recognition does **not** create an agency, reseller, franchise or employment relationship. Partners act independently, contract directly with their own clients, and speak for themselves rather than for Kimai. {% endcapture %} {{ text|markdownify }}
